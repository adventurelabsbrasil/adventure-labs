# Young Talents ATS

**Cliente:** Young Empreendimentos · **Owner GitHub:** [adventurelabsbrasil](https://github.com/adventurelabsbrasil) · **Repositório:** [young-talents](https://github.com/adventurelabsbrasil/young-talents)
